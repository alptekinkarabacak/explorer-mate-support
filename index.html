Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I book a guide?

Browse guides on the map, tap a pin to view their profile, and click "Book Tour" to select your dates.

Q: How do payments work?

Currently, payments are arranged directly between travelers and guides. Online payment features are coming soon!

Q: Can I message guides before booking?

Yes! Use the messaging feature to discuss your interests and ask questions before booking.

Privacy & Terms

Explorer Mate is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only essential information needed to provide our services:
